About Us

We are a small family committed to providing the best quality cinnamon rolls in Pittsburgh.

We started the shop back in 2016, but we have been baking the original cinnamon rolls for many years, as the recipe has been passed down for three generations and counting. We have decided to create a shop not only to highlight the fantastic original cinnamon roll, but also create various different flavors of cinnamon rolls and different glazes to appeal to more people in the Pittsburgh Area.

Our cinnamon rolls are made only with the highest quality ingredients and with lots of love. We want all of our customers to get the freshest cinnamon rolls they can get.

If you want learn more about the history of these cinnamon rolls and how much the business, feel free to chat with us in store.
